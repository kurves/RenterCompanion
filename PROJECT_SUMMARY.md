# RenterCompanion — Project Summary
RenterCompanion is a renter-side copilot that helps households prepare for a Low-Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) application in the Boston–Cambridge–Newton metro. Instead of guessing what documents are needed or whether they qualify, renters get a transparent, step-by-step guide that keeps them in control of their data and never decides eligibility for them.
The app walks renters through three stages. **Profile** lets them upload synthetic pay stubs or benefit letters, extracts only allowlisted fields, shows the exact source snippet and confidence for each value, and requires the renter to confirm or correct every field before it is reused. **Understand** answers rules questions with authoritative citations, displays deterministic income-limit calculations, and surfaces uncertainty or abstention when inputs or rules are unclear. **Prepare** flags missing or expired documents against a gold checklist and lets the renter preview, edit, download, and delete their readiness packet — with no automatic sending to properties or providers.
Built-in safety controls include live refusal, prompt-injection, and session-deletion tests; consent logging; and a "no decisioning" design that defers all eligibility judgments to qualified humans. By focusing on one metro, one program, and one rule year, RenterCompanion trades broad coverage for depth, correctness, and a genuinely accessible journey that respects renter privacy.
